Dolmas

Dolmas are as delicious and satisfying as they are simple

Common vegetables for stuffing are tomato, pepper, onion, zucchini, eggplant and pointed gourd. Dolmas prepared with olive oil and stuffed with rice are generally served cold with a garlic-yogurt sauce.

Porotos Granados

Built of New World ingredients, porotos granados is a stew of pumpkin, cranberry beans, corn, onion, spices and broth.

The final consistency is much like porridge, with the squash mashed into a purée. Flavor can be enhanced by roasting the corn over a flame first, and caramelizing the onions in the pot before adding the broth also enriches the dish.

Mohinga noodles

This rice-noodle and fish soup is considered by many to be the national dish of Myanmar. Though usually eaten for breakfast, it can be found anywhere at any time of day.

While the recipe varies from region to region, the foundation of the soup is chickpea flour, lemongrass, garlic, onion and ginger poured over rice vermicelli with lime and spicy chili.

White truffles

The firm flesh of a white truffle is pale cream to light brown in color, with white marbling throughout.

Truffles are the true fruit of the earth, more rare and precious than any other edible root, tuber or mushroom. There is no other flavor like them on earth, which is perhaps why they are so often described as heavenly.
